Medical Device & Equipment Supply

Medical supplies and equipment are a significant asset in the delivery of healthcare and must be managed effectively. Orders for POM drugs and OTC goods can be processed by Yazan. Also, we provide and keep up a variety of medical tools, first aid kits, and on-site medical investigation kits.

Fitness to Work Assessments

he goal of the pre-employment medical examination is to place and keep people in work environments that are compatible with their physical and mental capabilities.

Services for Integrated Remote Healthcare & On-Site Clinics

Remote workers are the focus of health initiatives involving prevention, diagnosis, and treatment. When it comes to hiring for the remote worksite, we only take into consideration qualified medical professionals with a strong commitment to patient-centered care and a focus on emergency response.

Training & Medical Staffing

When needed, Yazan offers its clients access to doctors, nurses, ambulance drivers, and other medical personnel. The employees are fully qualified in their respective disciplines. For doctors, medical residents, medical students, and other healthcare professionals, Yazan offers a variety of certificate programs.

Medevac, Patient Transport and Evacuations

We guarantee that you will obtain the most cost-effective evacuation options without sacrificing quality thanks to our years of expertise, competence, and knowledge in medevac and mass evacuations. Yazan is an expert in the medical, logistical, and regulatory procedures required to relocate patients in a secure, effective, and economical way.

24/7 Emergency Ambulance

To give patients, the finest care possible, Yazan has a fleet of modern emergency ambulances that are outfitted to the highest standards. Ambulances that are fully outfitted, licensed, and always have a professional ambulance driver on-site.
