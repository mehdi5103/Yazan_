About Us

Yazan for Medical Services Ltd. is a privately owned and managed company headquartered in As Sulaymaniyah, Iraqi Kurdistan. It was established in 2012 by healthcare professionals and experienced HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) management seniors. The company's primary goal is to develop healthcare facilities in significant areas of the healthcare sector and provide integrated healthcare services for both international and local companies operating in Iraq.

Yazan aims to cover all aspects of the healthcare industry by constructing facilities in key areas. This comprehensive approach allows them to offer integrated healthcare services, ensuring a holistic and seamless experience for their clients. Whether it's establishing hospitals, clinics, or other medical facilities, Yazan strives to provide high-quality healthcare services tailored to the needs of their clients.

Initiation

In the initiation phase, the company assesses the feasibility of the project, identifies potential risks and challenges, and establishes a clear project scope.

Planning

Yazan develops a detailed plan outlining the project's objectives, timelines, resource allocation, and budget requirements.

Product Selection

In this phase, the company assists in selecting the necessary medical products and equipment based on the project requirements and industry standards.

Execution

Once the planning and product selection are complete, Yazan oversees the implementation of the project, coordinating various stakeholders and ensuring smooth execution.

Monitoring & Control

Throughout the project's execution, Yazan closely monitors progress, tracks milestones, manages risks, and ensures compliance with quality standards and regulations.

Closure

Upon successful completion of the project, Yazan facilitates the closure phase, which involves documentation, finalizing financial aspects, and conducting a comprehensive review to evaluate the project's outcomes.

Our Approach

At Yazan for Medical Services Ltd., we understand the critical importance of prioritizing the well-being and safety of your employees. We recognize that maintaining a healthy workforce is essential for the smooth operation and growth of your business, particularly in the context of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. Even minor health or security hazards, if neglected, can escalate into significant issues, disrupting your core operations. Our aim is to ensure the safety and health of your workers so that you can focus on your primary tasks.

We acknowledge that each location has unique requirements concerning the health, safety, security, and general well-being of employees. There is no one-size-fits-all approach to guaranteeing their welfare. Therefore, our strategy is consultative in nature. Whether it's a corporate office or a remote oil rig, we collaborate with you to assess the risks associated with each working environment. Based on this assessment, we develop reliable and specialized preventive programs to protect your workforce.

In situations where prevention is not possible, we provide you and your employees with access to our 24/7 Help Service. This service is available to address any concerns related to pandemics, safety issues, or mental health problems affecting your employees. Our teams consist of multilingual medical professionals, nurses, security professionals, and logistical specialists who are ready to assist you whenever the need arises.

We consider ourselves your trusted advisor, assisting you in establishing vital workforce resilience and preparing for any contingency. Our primary objective is to safeguard your employees, which can result in cost savings, reduced insurance claims, and support for your Duty of Care and sustainability initiatives. By prioritizing health and well-being, your employees, your organization, and your reputation all stand to benefit. We are enthusiastic about the opportunity to work with you and contribute to your success.

Should you choose to partner with Yazan for Medical Services Ltd., we commit to providing comprehensive support, guidance, and expertise to ensure the health, safety, and well-being of your workforce.
