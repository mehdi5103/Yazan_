About Us

Yazan for Medical Services Ltd. is a privately owned and managed company, established in Iraqi-Kurdistan in 2010. the company was founded by healthcare professionals and HSE managements Seniors with decade of expertise in the field to develop facilities in all significant areas of the healthcare sector and providing integrated healthcare services for international and local companies working in Iraq.

By constructing facilities in all the key areas of the healthcare industry, Yazan seeks to provide integrated healthcare services. We provide thorough services for all the stage of your project's life cycle including seven phases of intake, initiation, planning, product selection, execution, monitoring & control, and closure
